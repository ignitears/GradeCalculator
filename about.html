About Us

Making grade calculations simple for Thai students.

Welcome to the Thai GPA Calculator. This tool was built to help students easily track, manage, and project their academic performance using the standard Thai 4.0 grading system.

We understand that manually calculating grades can be tedious and prone to errors. Our goal is to provide a fast, secure, and user-friendly interface where you can upload your data directly from Excel or copy-paste it from your school's portal.

All calculations are performed locally in your web browser. We do not store, save, or track your personal academic data on our servers.
